Hello

My name is Gunnar Magnussen and I am currently studying towards my MSc in Games on the technology track at ITU, which I will finish in the summer of 2025.

My main programming languages are C++ and C# and my areas of expertice are player movement and graphics. I have a lot of experience with Unity, but am also familiar with other engines, such as Unreal.

I am a creative person with a technical skillset and a passion for making games. I've been making games almost as long as I have been playing them. I'm a quick learner and try to always stay in the beginner's mindset to never miss opportunities to learn something new.
